PART 3 (3 or 4 minutes)
You both discuss a decision-making / problem-solving task, illustrated by visual
material, without interruption by the examiner.
Freetime Activities (Prioritise and discuss)
Turn to pictures 1-5 on page 131 taken from a leaflet about a one-month summer
course to study English in England. It shows some of the things that students could do
when classes have finished. If only three of these activities can be provided, which do
you think students would prefer?
